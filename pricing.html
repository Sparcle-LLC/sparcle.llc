Questions?

What does "BYO LLM" mean?

Bring Your Own LLM — you supply API keys from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Azure, or even run local models. You control the model, the cost, and the data flow. Available on all plans. Bundle and Complete also include managed AI if you prefer zero setup.

How does Bolt connect to our tools?

Bolt uses the open Model Context Protocol (MCP) to connect to 60+ SaaS tools your organization already uses — Slack, Jira, Notion, Salesforce, and more. No data migration needed.

What happens if we exceed the AI query limit?

You'll never be cut off. Overages are billed at a transparent per-query rate. We'll alert you before you approach limits. Contact us for details on fair-use thresholds and custom allowances.

Is there a pilot program?

Yes — we offer a 30-day production-grade pilot. Contact us for details on pilot pricing and how credits are applied toward your first annual contract.

Where does our data live?

With Absolute — entirely in your environment. With Bundle — your infra + Bolt's AI gateway (covered by DPA, zero-retention). With Complete — Bolt-managed infrastructure with your choice of region (US, EU, APAC).

Can we switch plans later?

Yes. Start with Absolute (BYO LLM) and upgrade to Bundle or Complete anytime. Your data, integrations, and configuration carry over seamlessly.
